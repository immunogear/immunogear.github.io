Brief Bio:

I have joined Inception Institute of Artificial Intelligence (IIAI), Abu Dhabi, UAE, as a Senior Scientist. I received the Ph.D. degree in computer science from Tianjin University, China, in 2013. After graduation, I spent about two years at Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore, as a Research Fellow. In 2015 to 2018, I worked as a Research Scientist at the Institute for Infocomm Research (I2R), Agency for Science, Technology and Research (A*STAR), Singapore.
